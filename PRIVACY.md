# Privacy Policy — Tabletop Tracker

**Last updated:** 18 June 2026

Tabletop Tracker ("the app") is designed to be private by default. Your
collection lives on your device, and we do not run servers that collect or
store your data.

## What data the app stores

Everything you enter — your games, photos, ratings, notes, house rules, plays,
players, loans, tags and expansions — is stored **only on your device**, in the
app's local database and file storage. We have no account system and no copy of
your data.

## When data leaves your device

Data only leaves your device when **you** choose an action that sends it:

- **BoardGameGeek lookup.** If you tap to look up a game, the app sends just the
  **game title (or barcode you typed)** to BoardGameGeek's public API to fetch
  details such as players, play time and the BGG rating. No personal
  information is sent. See BoardGameGeek's own privacy policy for how they
  handle requests.
- **Backup / CSV export.** If you export a backup or CSV, the app creates a file
  and hands it to your device's share sheet. Where that file goes (email, cloud
  drive, etc.) is entirely your choice; the app does not upload it anywhere.

The app does **not** use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs
that collect personal data, and it does **not** sell or share your data.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — used only if you choose to take a photo of a game.
- **Photo library** — used only if you choose to add a photo from your library.

Photos you add are copied into the app's private storage on your device.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including
children. It stores only the game data you choose to enter.

## Deleting your data

You can delete any game (and its photo) inside the app, or remove all data by
uninstalling the app from your device.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new
"last updated" date.
